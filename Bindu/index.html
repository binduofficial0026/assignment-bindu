Smarter Financing for the Smartphone Era

Datacultr’s Odyssey platform enables lenders to offer flexible mobile financing in emerging markets by turning financed devices into secure collateral.

Key Features That Drive Results

Device Locking Technology

Customizable locking options, device lock, block, and network lock designed for varied use cases across OEMs, telcos, NBFCs, and banks.

Factory Reset Protection

Odyssey always remains active and functional even after a factory reset and cannot be removed during the entire loan period.

Seamless Compatibility

Odyssey supports all Android devices — smartphones, tablets, and smart TVs — and fits smoothly into all phone financing journeys.

Customer First Approach

Send timely repayment reminders and unlock instructions directly to the device, keeping borrowers informed and engaged.

Why Businesses Trust Odyssey

100%

Contact ability

Messages are sent directly to the financed device, ensuring reach even if the SIM is swapped or the number is changed.

Action ability

Each message is designed to drive borrower action, whether it's making a payment, responding to terms, or re-engaging after inactivity.

Deliver ability

Every communication is tracked and timestamped, giving lenders total confidence that messages aren’t just sent but received.
